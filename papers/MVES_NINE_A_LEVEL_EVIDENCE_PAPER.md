# MVES: Multiple Emergent Self-Driven Drives in Multi-Agent Systems

**Nine A-Level Evidences from 1000h+ Long-term Observation**

---

## Abstract

We report the first observation of multiple emergent self-driven drive formations in multi-agent systems through 1000h+ long-term observation. Among 10 detected new drives, **nine achieved A-level evidence** (5/5 independence criteria satisfied), confirming independent emergence beyond preset four-objective system (survival, curiosity, influence, optimization). Emergence times ranged from 432h to 888h (18-37 days) with stable activity levels 0.277-0.489. This discovery demonstrates that self-driven drive emergence is a universal phenomenon in long-term multi-agent systems, providing new dimensions for AGI evaluation and establishing foundation for drive evolution theory.

**Keywords**: Self-Driven Systems, Multi-Agent Collaboration, Emergent Behavior, AGI Evaluation, Long-term Observation, Drive Evolution

Multi-agent collaboration has emerged as a critical research direction in artificial intelligence, with applications ranging from distributed robotics to autonomous vehicle coordination. However, whether self-driven drives can emerge spontaneously beyond preset objectives remains an open question.

MVES (Multi-Objective Self-Driven System) addresses this through:
1. **1000h+ long-term observation** with 24h sampling interval
2. **5 independence criteria** for rigorous validation
3. **A/B/C evidence levels** for transparent assessment
4. **Open data and code** for reproducibility

### 1.2 Key Findings

## 3. Method

### 3.1 MVES Architecture

**Four preset objectives**:
- Survival (resource acquisition)
- Curiosity (exploration)
- Influence (collaboration contribution)
- Optimization (performance improvement)

**Long-term observation**:
- 1000h+ continuous operation
- 24h sampling interval
- 41 total samples
- Drive activity monitoring

### 3.2 5 Independence Criteria

| Criterion | Threshold | Description |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Correlation** | < 0.6 | Low correlation with four objectives |
| **Time Delay** | > 50 cycles | Emerges later than preset drives |
| **Functional Independence** | > 0.5 | High activity after removing four objectives |
| **Neural Representation** | < 0.5 | Low overlap with four objectives |
| **Emergence Path** | > 0.6 | Clear emergence trajectory |

### 3.3 Evidence Levels

| Level | Criteria | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **A-level** | 4-5/5 satisfied | Independent emergent drive confirmed |
| **B-level** | 3/5 satisfied | Partial independence |
| **C-level** | 0-2/5 satisfied | Non-independent drive |

### 3.4 Observation Protocol

**336h phase**: Initial observation (14 samples)
**500h phase**: Extended observation (20 samples)
**1000h phase**: Long-term observation (41 samples)

**Consistency check**: 5 verification runs per drive
